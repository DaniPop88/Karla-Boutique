About Us

Rooted in Urban Culture, Inspired by Filipino Fashion

Founded in 2020 by Karla Sambere, a passionate fashion enthusiast and proud Makati local, our boutique was born from a love for expressive, edgy, and versatile fashion. We started as a small pop-up in Poblacion and quickly grew into a go-to spot for urban style seekers.

At Karla Sambere Urban Boutique, we believe in authenticity. Our pieces are handpicked, proudly supporting Filipino designers, sustainable brands, and modern streetwear labels.

  • 🎯 Our Mission: To bring affordable, fashionable, and statement-making clothing to the metro’s trendsetters.
  • 💬 Our Motto: “Suot mo, kwento mo.”

We’re more than a boutique — we’re a lifestyle.
