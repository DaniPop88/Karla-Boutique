Refund & Exchange Policy

Our aim is to ensure you are genuinely happy with every purchase. If you need to return or exchange an item, we're here to make the process straightforward and hassle-free.

Eligibility for Refunds & Exchanges

  • Items may be returned or exchanged within 7 days of receipt.
  • Products must be unused, in original condition, and have all tags attached.
  • Sale items, accessories, and footwear may only be exchanged if they are defective or incorrect.
  • Personalized or custom items are non-refundable unless faulty.

How to Request a Refund or Exchange

  • Email contact@karlasambere-boutique.com or message us on Viber/Instagram with your order number and reason for return.
  • Our team will guide you through the next steps and provide a return address if eligible.
  • Shipping fees for returns are shouldered by the customer, unless the item is defective or incorrect.

Refund Process

  • Once your return is received and inspected, we'll send a confirmation email.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days via your original payment method.
  • Exchanges are shipped out as soon as the returned item is verified.
  • If your return does not meet eligibility, we will notify you and arrange to send the item back.

Important Notes

  • We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet policy requirements.
  • Refund or exchange requests submitted after the 7-day window may not be accepted.

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns, please reach out to our customer care team. We're always happy to help ensure your shopping experience is smooth and enjoyable.
