Privacy Policy

At Karla Sambere Urban Boutique, we respect your privacy and take your personal information seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data whenever you visit our website, shop in-store, or connect with us online.

What Information We Collect

We gather only the details needed to deliver great service and fulfill your orders. This may include:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Order history and preferences
  • Payment details (processed securely and never stored by us)
  • Communications, feedback, or inquiries you share with us

How We Use Your Information

  • To process orders and provide customer support
  • To notify you about order updates, promotions, or boutique news (if you opt in)
  • To personalize your shopping experience and improve our products
  • To comply with legal requirements and prevent fraud

How We Protect Your Data

We use up-to-date security measures to safeguard your personal details. Access is limited to authorized staff, and all transactions are encrypted using secure protocols.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your data to third parties. Information is shared only with trusted partners (such as couriers or payment providers) to complete your transaction.

Your Rights & Choices

  • You may access, update, or request deletion of your personal information at any time.
  • You can opt out of promotional communications by contacting us or using the unsubscribe link in emails.
  • For privacy requests, reach us at contact@karlasambere-boutique.com or call +63 908 123 4765.

Policy Updates

We may revise this policy as needed to reflect changes in our practices or Philippine regulations. Updates will be posted on this page, and significant changes will be communicated to you directly.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, please reach out. Our team is here to help.
